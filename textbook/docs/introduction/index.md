# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics. This textbook is designed to provide you with an in-depth understanding of the intersection between artificial intelligence and robotics, with a focus on humanoid systems.

## About This Textbook

This textbook covers various aspects of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics through four major modules:

1. **ROS 2** - Robot Operating System version 2
2. **Gazebo / Unity Simulation** - Simulation environments for robotics
3. **NVIDIA Isaac Robotics** - Advanced robotics platform
4. **Vision-Language-Action (VLA) Systems** - Integration of perception, reasoning, and action

Each module is designed to build upon the previous ones, creating a comprehensive learning journey through the field of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this textbook, you will:
- Understand the fundamentals of robotics and AI integration
- Learn to work with state-of-the-art robotics platforms
- Gain experience with simulation environments
- Explore advanced VLA systems
- Apply AI techniques to real-world robotics problems
